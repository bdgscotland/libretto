Introduction

I have 2 Toshiba Librettos, the older 50CT and the newer 100CT. I have a few accessories which make working with them slightly easier, the plan of this site is to provide everything I learned during the process of reviving these from the dead. Oh, and any fun things I found along the way.

I expect to have hardware info, tweaks/mods, administrative items, and also links to drivers/softwares, and how-tos. Most of the info online is old, so I plan on consolidating it here and giving credit where it's available.
